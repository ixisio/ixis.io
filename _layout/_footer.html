About Andreas Klein

As a freelance Frontend Developer, I create accessible and intuitive Web-Interfaces for Humans.

I help Agencies and Companies to realize large scale projects with a focus on Responsive Webdesign, Accessibility, Webstandards, Performance, Coding-Guidelines, Styleguides, Workflows and choosing the right Tools and Frameworks. Outside of the web, I’m a proud father of two beautiful children, a passionate freestyle skateboarder/snowboarder and I like the taste of good espresso.

If there is still time, I write on my personal blog about Frontend Stuff, Accessibility and Node.js programming.
